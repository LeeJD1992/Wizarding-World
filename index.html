The Wizarding World of Harry Potter

The Wizarding World created by J.K Rowling has become one of the most interesting and intriguing worlds created in literature and film. Beginning with Harry Potter and the Philosophers Stone first published in 1997, the Wizarding World has expanded to a further 6 Harry Potter books. These books have been adapted into 7 movies, beginning in 2001, the Harry Potter series has become a worldwide phenomenon with millions of fans worldwide.
